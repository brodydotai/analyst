## Assessor Notes

The report is comprehensive and follows the Specialty & Advanced Materials playbook structure closely. The core insight — that CLF's GOES monopoly is mispriced inside a commodity steel wrapper — is well-supported with tariff data, DOE standards analysis, and competitive benchmarking. The primary gap is the absence of a formal DCF sensitivity matrix, replaced with a sum-of-parts + multiple expansion approach that is arguably more appropriate for a levered cyclical company. The 5 IDPs are well-identified and actionable. The 3 Investigation Tracks have clear hypotheses and data requirements. Data confidence appropriately flagged at 0.60 given GOES segment opacity.
