## Scoring Rationale

### Strengths

1. **Comprehensive Material Classification:** Report clearly distinguishes 3 material families with specific regulatory/strategic drivers and value chain positioning.

2. **Rigorous Specification Moat Analysis:** Qualification cycles (12-36 months HVAC, 24-60 months nuclear), switching costs ($5-20M), and sole-source positions (Metropolis) well-documented.

3. **Detailed Valuation Framework:** DCF with sensitivity cases (Base/Bear/Bull), peer comparison (EV/EBITDA, P/E, ROIC), and implied price targets ($102-200/share).

4. **Robust IDP Flagging:** 6 IDPs identified with clear materiality assessments and high-agency follow-up requirements (patent cliff, backlog validation, Spectra adoption, etc.).

5. **High-Agency Investigation Tracks:** 3 tracks with explicit methodology, expected outcomes, and downside thresholds for nuclear, patents, and Spectra.

6. **Balance Sheet & Capital Allocation:** Net debt, leverage, CapEx plans, and cash flow mechanics clearly outlined.

7. **Competitive Landscape:** Direct competitors named by segment (Chemours, Arkema, Daikin, DSM) with market share estimates and substitution risks identified.

8. **End-Market Diversification:** Revenue mapped across RAS (72%) and ESM (28%), with cyclicality drivers (interest rates, semiconductor capex) noted.

### Gaps & Weaknesses

1. **Leadership Pedigree Missing:** No detail on CEO/leadership materials science expertise, insider share ownership %, board composition, or insider transaction history (Playbook A2).

2. **Sustainability Positioning Underdeveloped:** ESG/sustainability reporting and customer requirement alignment (especially aerospace/defense) not discussed (Playbook F3).

3. **Gross Margin Decomposition Incomplete:** Scrap rates, yield metrics, and value-add processing layers not detailed (Playbook B4).

4. **Customer Concentration Quantification Gaps:** Top 5 customer % estimated for RAS (40-60%) but not explicitly disclosed; ESM concentration not addressed.

5. **Tariff/Trade Policy Detail Sparse:** Section 232/301 exposure and supply chain friend-shoring quantification not explicit (Playbook E3).

6. **Working Capital & Inventory Strategy:** Carrying costs, strategic inventory holdings, and working capital cycle not explicitly detailed (Playbook B3).
